Northern Blossom Flower Farm

Northern Blossom Flower Farm in Atok is known for its colorful flower fields and cool mountain climate. It offers a relaxing view of nature and is perfect for photography and sightseeing.

  • Location: Atok, Benguet
  • Best Time: December – February
  • Tip: Visit early morning for best lighting and fewer crowds
