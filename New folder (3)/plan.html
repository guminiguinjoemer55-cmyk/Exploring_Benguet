Plan Your Trip

Make your visit to Benguet smooth and enjoyable with this simple travel guide.

📅 Sample 1-Day Itinerary

  • Morning: Visit Strawberry Farm and pick fresh strawberries
  • Afternoon: Explore Northern Blossom Flower Farm
  • Evening: Relax and enjoy local food in town

🎒 What to Bring

  • Jacket or sweater (weather is cold)
  • Comfortable shoes for walking
  • Water bottle
  • Camera or smartphone

🚌 Transportation Tips

  • Ride a bus or jeepney going to Benguet
  • Plan your route ahead of time
  • Travel early to avoid traffic

💰 Estimated Budget

₱1000 – ₱2000 per person depending on transportation, food, and entrance fees.
