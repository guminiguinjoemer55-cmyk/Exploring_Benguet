Strawberry Farm

The Strawberry Farm in La Trinidad is one of the most popular tourist spots in Benguet. Visitors can pick fresh strawberries, enjoy local products, and experience farm life.

  • Location: La Trinidad, Benguet
  • Best Time: November – May
  • Tip: Visit early to avoid crowds and get fresh strawberries
